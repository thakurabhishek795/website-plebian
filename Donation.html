Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations to Plebian Collective are 50% tax-exempt under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act.

Will I receive a receipt?

Yes, an automated receipt will be sent to your registered email address immediately after the successful transaction.

Can I donate specifically for a cause?

Absolutely. You can write to us at support@plebian.org after donating to specify if you want your funds used for a specific program (e.g., Education, Environment).
